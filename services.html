Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need a technical background to work with you?
Not at all. I translate complex technical concepts into plain language. You describe the business problem — I handle all the engineering. Regular check-ins ensure you always know what's being built.
How long does a typical project take?
Simple automations take 3–7 days. Medium projects (data pipeline, single agent) are typically 2–4 weeks. Large systems with multiple integrations range from 4–10 weeks. I'll give a firm timeline in the proposal.
What happens after delivery?
Every project includes documentation and a walkthrough session. Professional and Enterprise engagements include post-delivery support. I'm also available for ongoing maintenance retainers if needed.
Do you work with existing systems or only greenfield builds?
Both. I frequently integrate with existing ERP systems, CRMs, databases, and cloud environments. I assess your current stack during the discovery phase and design around what you already have.
Are the prices on this page fixed?
The pricing tiers are guides. Every project is different — some simpler, some more complex. After our discovery call I'll give you an exact quote with no hidden costs.
